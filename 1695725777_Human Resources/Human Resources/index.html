Recruitment and Hiring

HR professionals are responsible for attracting, sourcing, and hiring new employees. This includes posting job vacancies, conducting interviews, and managing the onboarding process.

Employee Relations

HR handles employee-related issues, such as conflicts, grievances, and disputes. They also promote a positive workplace culture and address concerns to maintain a harmonious work environment.

Training and Development

HR designs and implements training programs to enhance employees' skills and knowledge. This can include both initial onboarding training and ongoing professional development.
